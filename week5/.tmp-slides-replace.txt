## Matrix Representation
- Represent the system as $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$
- Coefficient matrix $A$, variable vector $\mathbf{x}$, constant vector $\mathbf{b}$
- This form generalizes solving systems to higher dimensions

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
2 & -1
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\[4pt]
y
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
5 \\[4pt]
1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

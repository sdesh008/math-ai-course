Interactive: Solutions of Two Linear Equations

Adjust coefficients for two equations of the form a x + b y = c and d x + e y = f. The plot shows the two lines and their intersection (if any).

Intersection: ( — )   |   Status: —

Matrix form: $A\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}$ with $A=\begin{pmatrix}a&b\\d&e\end{pmatrix}$ and $\mathbf{b}=(c,f)^T$. The system is solvable if $\det(A)\neq0$ (unique solution). If $\det(A)=0$, either no solution or infinitely many exist depending on consistency.
